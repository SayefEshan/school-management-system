### **Landlord App User Flow**

**Start:** The Landlord App streamlines property management, tenant interactions, and administrative tasks for landlords, ensuring efficiency and security throughout their experience.

#### **Steps:**

### **1\. Onboarding**

**Start:** The landlord downloads and opens the app.

- **Role Selection**
  - Choose the "Landlord" role.
- **Account Creation**
  - Sign up with the following details:
    - Name
    - Email (with email verification)
    - Phone number (with OTP verification)
    - Password
- **Identity Verification**
  - It is essential to prevent fraud and fake property listings.
  - Verify with government-issued ID:
    - Driver's License or Passport.

Note: Landlords cannot access the platform without completing identity verification.

- **Subscription Plan Selection**
  - Choose a plan:
    - **Basic (Free):** No property verification is required.
    - **Premium (Paid):** Additional features like property verification, agent assignment, and more.
- **Property Ownership Verification** (For Premium Plans)
  - Upload documents:
    - Land Data Registry certificate.
    - Certificate of Landlord Insurance.
  - Example: John uploads a scanned copy of his property ownership certificate.
- **Property Information**
  - Automatic prefill of property information from Title Registry.
  - Mandatory uploads:
    - Property photos.
    - Property videos (optional for Silver; required for Platinum subscriptions).
  - Additional steps:
    - Assign an agent (optional for Silver and Platinum plans).
    - Create a Property Maintenance Profile with:
      - Last date of fire alarm service.
      - Electrical Compliance Certificate.
      - Plumbing Compliance Certificate (optional).
      - Owner's signed declaration.
- **Completion Notification**
  - Message: "Your account has been successfully created."

**End:** Landlord profile is set up.

<br/>**2\. Agent Creation (Need Admin Approval)**

**Start:** The landlord navigates to the agent creation section.

#### **Steps:**

- **Assign a Property Agent:**
  - Enter or select:
    - Agent Name.
    - Agency Name.
    - Agent's REIA Membership Number (mandatory for compliance).
  - Subscription Requirement:
    - Basic: Limited to 5 properties; cannot assign agents.
    - Silver/Platinum: Unlimited properties and the ability to assign agents or create new agents.
- **Add Agent Details:**
  - Upload documents for verification (if applicable):
    - License or REIA Certification.
    - Signed contract (optional for record-keeping).
- **Agent Management:**
  - Replace agents or modify their assigned properties from this section.
  - Notify agents about assignments or modifications through in-app notifications or email.

**Notification:** "Agent successfully assigned to \[Property Name\]."  
**End:** Agent is added to manage specific properties.

### **3\. Property Management**

**Start:** Landlord accesses the Property Management section. \[The subscriptions also limit the number of properties the owners are allowed to list. Anything over 5 will need to be assigned to an agent - thereby they cannot manage them with Basic subscriptions}

- **Add a New Property**
  - Steps include:
    - Upload/Edit property images.
    - Add rent amount (with recommended value suggestions).
    - Specify inspection options (e.g., In-person or AR).
    - Create a Condition Report: Upload detailed photos for each room/area with notes.
    - Assign an agent (REIA Membership Number required).
    - Prepare a Tenant Handover Checklist to be signed when keys are handed over.
  - Example: John lists a 2-bedroom apartment for \$550/week with in-person inspections enabled.
- **Tenant Matches**
  - Example: "Tenant Sarah matches your property at 15 Collins St."
- **Review Tenant Applications**
  - Approve or reject tenant applications.
  - Example: John reviews Sarah's application and approves her for the property.

**End:** Property is rented, and tenants are notified.

### **4\. Payment Management**

**Start:** Landlord navigates to payment details.

- **Upcoming Payments**
  - Example: "Feb 1, 2025 - \$550 (Tenant: Sarah)."
- **Payment History**
  - Chronologically listed payments.
  - Example: "Jan 1, 2025 - \$550 Paid."
- **Generate Payment Reports**
  - Export payments as CSV files, view monthly summaries, and analyze trends.
  - Notification: "Rent payment for Feb 1, 2025, has been received."

**End:** Payments are tracked efficiently.

### **5\. Repairs and Maintenance**

**Start:** Landlord accesses tenant repair requests.

- **View Repair Requests**
  - Example: "Leaking faucet - Tenant Sarah."
- **Assign Tradespeople**
  - Assign a professional to handle repairs.
  - Example: John assigns Mike's Plumbing to resolve a leaking faucet.
- **Repair Notifications**
  - To tenant: "Repair assigned to Mike's Plumbing."
  - To landlord: "Repair completed by Mike's Plumbing."

**End:** Repairs are resolved, and feedback is recorded.

### **6\. Dashboard Overview**

**Start:** The dashboard offers actionable insights for landlords.

- **Welcome Section**
  - Personalized greeting with updates.
  - Example: "Welcome, John! You have 2 new tenant applications to review."
  - Can View any Issues raised by Tenant (High-Priority)
- **Revenue Overview**
  - Monthly and lifetime earnings with a property breakdown.
  - Example:
    - _Jan 2025:_ \$2,200 (4 properties).
    - _Lifetime Revenue:_ \$48,000.
    - _By Property:_
      - 15 Collins St: \$550/month - Tenant: Sarah.
      - 10 Riverdale Lane: \$600/month - Tenant: Mike.
- **Tenant Applications**
  - View pending and approved applications.
  - Example:
    - _Pending:_ "Sarah has applied for 20 Green St."
    - _Approved:_ "John approved Sarah for 15 Collins St on Jan 15, 2025."
- **Repair Management**
  - Active Repairs: "Leaking faucet at 15 Collins St - Assigned to Mike's Plumbing."
  - Completed Repairs: "Broken window fixed at 10 Riverdale Lane on Jan 20, 2025."
- **Property Listings**
  - Active Listings: "15 Collins St - Rented to Sarah."
  - Expired Listings: "5 Park Ave - Expired on Dec 31, 2024."
- **Issue Mitigation**
  - Escalate legal disputes to VCAT.
  - Assign legal representatives for compliance or tenancy issues.
- **Notifications**
  - Example:
    - "Sarah's rent for Feb 1, 2025, has been processed."
    - "Tenant Mike requested a repair for 'Heating issue' on Jan 18, 2025."
    - You have an upcoming hearing for your property
    - Last date to submit your documents to VCAT administration.
